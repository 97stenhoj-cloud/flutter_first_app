## 2. WHAT ARE COOKIES?

### 2.1 Definition

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, smartphone, tablet) when you visit a website or use an application. They contain information that is transferred to your device's hard drive.

### 2.2 Purpose

Cookies help websites and apps:
- Remember your preferences and settings
- Understand how you use the service
- Improve user experience
- Analyze performance and traffic
- Deliver relevant content
- Maintain security

### 2.3 Types of Cookies by Lifespan

**Session Cookies:**
- Temporary cookies that expire when you close your browser
- Used for navigation and functionality during your visit
- Automatically deleted when you end your session

**Persistent Cookies:**
- Remain on your device for a set period or until manually deleted
- Remember your preferences across multiple visits
- Can last from days to years depending on the cookie

### 2.4 Types of Cookies by Origin

**First-Party Cookies:**
- Set directly by our website/app
- Used for core functionality and analytics
- Under our direct control

**Third-Party Cookies:**
- Set by external services (e.g., Google Ads, analytics providers)
- Used for advertising, analytics, or social media integration
- Governed by third-party privacy policies

## 3. COOKIES WE USE

### 3.1 Mobile App Cookies

The Connect mobile app uses **minimal cookies and local storage:**

#### Strictly Necessary (No Consent Required)

**Authentication Tokens:**
- **Purpose:** Keep you logged in to your account
- **Type:** First-party, session & persistent
- **Duration:** 30 days or until logout
- **Data Stored:** Session tokens, user ID
- **Why Necessary:** Required for account security and access

**App Preferences:**
- **Purpose:** Remember your theme (dark/light) and language settings
- **Type:** First-party, persistent
- **Duration:** Until app uninstall or manual deletion
- **Data Stored:** Theme preference, locale settings
- **Why Necessary:** Provide personalized experience

**Offline Cache:**
- **Purpose:** Enable offline mode functionality
- **Type:** First-party, persistent
- **Duration:** Until overwritten or app uninstall
- **Data Stored:** Question cache, category data
- **Why Necessary:** Allow app usage without internet connection

#### Performance and Analytics (Consent-Based)

**Usage Analytics:**
- **Purpose:** Understand how features are used, improve app performance
- **Type:** First-party, persistent
- **Duration:** Until app uninstall
- **Data Stored:** Session duration, features accessed, errors encountered
- **Service:** Custom analytics via Supabase
- **Control:** Can be disabled in app settings

**Crash Reporting:**
- **Purpose:** Identify and fix bugs and crashes
- **Type:** First-party, session
- **Duration:** Until sent to server (typically <24 hours)
- **Data Stored:** Error logs, stack traces, device info
- **Control:** Can be disabled in app settings

- Does not identify you personally
- Resets when app is reinstalled

**Temporary Session Data:**
- Stored only during active app/website session
- Automatically deleted when session ends
- Used for: navigation state, form data, temporary preferences

## 6. LEGAL BASIS FOR COOKIES (GDPR)

Under EU law (GDPR and ePrivacy Directive), we use different legal bases for different cookie types:

### 6.1 Consent (Article 6(1)(a) GDPR)

**Cookies requiring consent:**
- Analytics cookies (Google Analytics)
- Advertising cookies (Google Ads)
- Non-essential functional cookies

**How we obtain consent:**
- Cookie banner on first website visit
- App installation and first launch (advertising only)
- Settings toggles for analytics

**Your right to withdraw:**
- Change website cookie settings via banner link
- Adjust app settings under "Settings > Privacy"
- Delete app cookies by clearing app data

### 6.2 Legitimate Interest (Article 6(1)(f) GDPR)

**Cookies based on legitimate interest:**
- Security cookies (CSRF protection, session management)
- Essential functionality cookies
- Cookie consent record

**Balancing test:**
Our legitimate interest in security and essential functionality outweighs any minimal privacy impact, as these cookies:
- Do not track you across websites
- Contain minimal personal data
- Are necessary for service operation
- Cannot reasonably be avoided while still providing the service

### 6.3 Contractual Necessity (Article 6(1)(b) GDPR)

**Cookies necessary for contract performance:**
- Authentication tokens (required to provide account features)
- Subscription status (required to deliver paid features)

These cookies are essential to fulfill our contractual obligations under the Terms of Service.

## 8. IMPACT OF BLOCKING COOKIES

### 8.1 Website Impact

If you block or delete cookies, you may experience:

**Strictly Necessary Cookies (if blocked):**
- Unable to log in to accounts
- Security vulnerabilities
- Website errors or broken functionality
- Loss of session data

**Functional Cookies (if blocked):**
- Language resets to default on each visit
- Need to accept cookie banner repeatedly
- Preferences not saved

**Analytics Cookies (if blocked):**
- No impact on functionality
- We receive less data about website usage
- May result in slower improvement of user experience

### 8.2 Mobile App Impact

**Essential Storage (if cleared):**
- Logged out of account
- Preferences reset to default
- Offline content deleted
- Custom decks may be lost (if not synced to cloud)

**Analytics Disabled:**
- No impact on app functionality
- We receive less usage data
- May take longer to identify and fix bugs

**Advertising (Free Tier):**
- Ads will still appear but may be less relevant
- Cannot block ads without premium subscription
